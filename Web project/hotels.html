MTR


Mavalli Tiffin Rooms’ journey begins in 1924, when three brothers decided to venture into the city’s then nascent F&B business. Today, MTR brings in the crowds from around the world and they have numerous outlets around the city. It’s like an old two-storeyed complex that has halls for various occasions and meals.

Most patrons visit MTR in the wee hours of the morning. Here, you can get your morning kick of some piping hot masala dosas doused in homemade ghee, blanketing a hearty onion-packed aloogadde palya. Their puffed up pooris resembling oxygen bags of bliss are a more greasy encounter, as they come with a gravy of potato sagu and chutney.


Mangalore Pearl


For people who craved for homely food, there were just a handful of people serving Mangalorean delicacies. That's when Steven Pinto quit his job in an IT firm to extend the hospitality of serving Mangalorean delicacies beyond his family and friends to the public who also loved seafood as much as he did. The restaurant has become a household name among Mangaloreans in Bangalore in particular and seafood lovers in general.

Mangalore is home to a variety of cultures. The people of the city are known for their warmth and welcoming spirit. This is the feeling one gets at Mangalore Pearl. The range of food includes seafood, chicken, mutton and pork cooked in different styles. At Mangalore Pearl, the aim is dual - to offer Mangaloreans a home away from home and to introduce the world at large to the tasty Mangalorean cuisine.




Miller's 46


A themed restaurant that looks to invoke the wild, wild west, Millers 46 has little room for the delicate in its decor and on its menu. . Heavy wooden chairs, wheels and wooden barrels hanging from the roof, showpiece animal skulls, and cowboy figurines standing resolutely in the corner – you’ll see it all.

The Big Boy Texas T-Bone Steak is one of their classics and a meat lover’s delight. What you get is a hunk of meat (a fillet and a strip – a type of cut that comes with a layer of fat on top – rendered together). Also, don’t even think about asking for a bite of our simple Fillet Mignon With Mushroom Sauce. Now, if you’re a chicken ‘steak’ sort, opt for the Jamaican Pepper one that really ups the spice, but without going overboard.


Corner Ice Cream House


Another eating joint that has founded itself only at the turn of the millennium, Corner House Ice Cream is a sweet delight that goes on doing what it does best in providing some of the best Hot Chocolate Fudges, and Brownie Sundaes in Bangalore.

Located in Kormangala in bangalore, this place is very famous for Hot Chocolate Fudge, Brownie Sundae, Apple Pies and many oyher delicious foods.




Olive's Kitchen


A beautiful evening and a peaceful dinner can be spent very well here. Its outdoor seating has a blissful ambience while the indoor one has a calm and cozy vibe. Both the areas being perfect for long conversations,they provide lip smacking meals and a good time with loved ones. Recommended - Hazelnut Crunch Cake, Red Sauce Pasta.


JW Kitchen


Located in the lavishing place of Bangalore, this place is very famous for live music, open air seating, and delicious food. Dozen Mezze, Mediterranean Kebab Platter, Signature Pomegranate Chicken are some foods which are worsth tasting at this place. All-day International restaurant offering an a la carte menu and Sunday brunch at a luxury hotel. JW kitchen has everything from soups, salads, starters to desi dishes and continental food as well. Their desserts are not to be missed out.




Brahmin’s Coffee Bar


The best in its class of traditional south Indian food can be found in the Brahmin’s Coffee Bar at Basavanagudi in Bangalore. It offers steamingly hot rice idlis which are soft and crispy vadas. They also serve a variety of dishes here but the local favorite is surely the Idly-Vada combo. Located in Basavanagudithis place is very popular for very very tasty food at very minimum cost.



Airlines Hotel


An old charm of Bangalore , it is always filled with crowd in the early hours of the day. Enjoying the morning breakfast under the shades of a Banyan tree gives a perfect start to the day. From South Indianto North Indian, the place caters every kind of dishes, making it great for foodies that like variety.
