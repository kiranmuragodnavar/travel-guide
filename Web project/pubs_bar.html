ABC


ABC or Arbor Brewing Company is not new to the world of brewing beer. The place offers creative American pub food and incredible variants in handcrafted beer. ABC at Bangalore sticks to its American ways and you step into a western tavern styled premise with all-wooden interiors. This is definitely the best place to hang out if you have been missing the Oktoberfest!

The warm and cozy ambiance of the place attracts hundreds of guests on weekends but if you want a cozy space for your group, make sure that you come early. Weekdays are your best bet to fall in love with the ambiance sans the crowd. It is one of the best pubs in Bangalore with a dance floor. ABC offers a full bar, DJ, Elevator, valet parking, outdoor seating and a smoking area

TOIT brews its own beer and this makes the place a favorite among aficionados.

District 6


District 6 is an upscale micro-brewery that offers both fine dining and classic brews. If you have been searching for a low profile, quaint but ambient place to have a glass of beer with your dinner, this is it.

It a place that is meant for conversations, be it with your partner or with a small group of friends. The restaurant also offers great music in a laid back ambience. If you are a music lover of any sort, be here.


F-Bar


The F-Bar is the newest lounge bar in the city and offers a cutting edge, contemporary design that has been immaculately fused with a touch of the old world. The property spreads across 17,000 sqft and has been built around folia that age somewhat about 100 years. With natural light seeping into the lounge area, you can expect something special.

The FBar is being deemed as the longest bar of South India and is a must visit. Another special highlight of the FBar is the cascading waterfall on one of the sides and this act as a ramp space for performance art and fashion shows. Itâ€™s a fancy setup but surely will blow your mind. Lastly, the place also offers a very authentic food experience from across the globe.




La Casa


La Casa is a place that is known for its highly authentic cuisine. The ingredients that you find in the food here is completely natural and this greatly affects the taste. The bar is also accompanied by a DJ that dishes out some great music.

Take a sip of their signature cocktail and head to the dance floor with your partner or friends. This is one of the most popular pubs in Bangalore for couples. La Casa is a very happening place if you have been looking for fun. The best part about the place is that the menu is quite pocket-friendly and also features a section for sea-food. The light hearted ambiance lifts up your mood in no time!


The Black Rabbit


The Black Rabbit comes as a very innovative food and dining space. The interiors are out of the box and you will immediately walk into warmth that makes up for your bad mood. There is a central communal table and smaller seating in many permutations across the spread of the restaurant.

The service in particular is stated to be exemplarity. The food and drinks are competitively priced and you can hope to have a good time with your friends here.




Hakuna Matata


Hakuna Matata has been a fairly new addition to the Bangalore nightlife but it has already made quite of an impact. The place has conveniently been a place for executives to unwind after work.

Hakuna Matata is open all day and you can hope to both work and play in it's fun ambiance. The urban hangout has been particularly designed to keep you connected. It has always been a preferred gathering for artists.Hakuna Matata serves an extensive range of Continental and Asian dishes along with a wide ray of drinks and cocktails. Do try out the seafood too.

The Bierre's Club


Centrally located in Bangalore, Biere Club is another micro brewery and you can expect a range of authentic and locally brewed drinks. Its wheat beer has been a particular favorite for more than a decade now and has somewhat acquired a legendary status. A high degree of sophistication goes into beer brewing and you can also expect a wide serving of food platters too. The Biere Club would also be a perfect stop if you are with your family. As understood, the place gets a very decent laid back crowd that loves its food and drinks. The rustic ambiance is complimented by soft music in the background and a very friendly staff.
